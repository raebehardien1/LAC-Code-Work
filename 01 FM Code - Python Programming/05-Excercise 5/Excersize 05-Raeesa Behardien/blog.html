Technology in phones and how it is used

A cell phone is a portable electronic device used for communication through voice calls, text messages, and internet access. It incorporates various technologies, such as computing, programming, and the internet, to enable wireless communication and access to a wide range of applications and services.They help us in making our lives easy and convenient. They help us communicate with our loved ones and carry out our work efficiently. Furthermore, they also do the work of the computer, calculator, and cameras.

Common use of Technology in phones

92.1% of households in South Africa own a working cell phone, up from 3.3% in 2001.Samsung is the most popular smartphone brand in South Africa, with a 50.92% market share in 2024. Apple is second with a 15.88% market share, and Huawei is third with a 14.35% market share.
